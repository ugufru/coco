TRS-80 Color Computer

This book is for anyone with a Color Computer and a sense of adventure. You don't need to know anything about Forth — or even about programming. Just sit down, follow along, and by the end you'll have written real programs that run on real hardware.

Table of Contents

  1. 1 Meet Your Stack — the foundation of everything in Forth
  2. 2 Say Something — printing text on your CoCo's screen
  3. 3 Make Your Own Words — colon definitions and building a vocabulary
  4. 4 The Stack Is Your Friend — DUP, DROP, SWAP, OVER
  5. 5 Remember Things — VARIABLE, @, !
  6. 6 Count and Loop — DO, LOOP, I
  7. 7 Decisions — IF, ELSE, THEN and comparison words
  8. 8 Read the Keyboard — KEY and interactive programs
  9. 9 Numbers on Screen — *, /MOD, and printing any number
  10. 10 The Calculator — BEGIN…AGAIN and an RPN calculator
  11. 11 Anywhere on Screen — AT and fixed screen layouts
  12. 12 The Guessing Game — a complete game using everything
  13. 13 Getting It onto Your CoCo — CoCoSDC, DriveWire, and cassette

What you'll need: A TRS-80 Color Computer (any model) or the XRoar emulator. A modern computer running Linux or macOS for cross-compiling. That's it.

Each chapter builds on the last. The programs get more interesting as you go.
